What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items with original tags attached. Returns are free for customers in the United States.

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the US. Express shipping is available for delivery in 2-3 business days.
What materials are your leggings made from?
Our leggings are made from a premium blend of nylon and spandex, providing optimal stretch, comfort, and durability for all types of yoga and fitness activities.

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination.
